Potential Phase 2: Social Roleplay Layer

If the identity layer gains meaningful adoption, a possible next phase would explore a social roleplay layer built on top of those identities.

This would not be a traditional social network. Instead, it would be a narrative-driven environment where users interact through roles, factions, and personas.

Unlike current platforms focused on real-world status, this layer would emphasize character, role, and storytelling over profiles and follower counts.

Gen Z already roleplays across games, fandoms, and Discord-based communities. This phase would formalize behavior that already exists.

This is a speculative future direction, not a committed roadmap. Phase 2 would only be explored if Phase 1 demonstrates strong organic engagement.
